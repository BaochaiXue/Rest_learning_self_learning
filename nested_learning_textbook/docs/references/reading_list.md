## Essential (Must Read Before Teaching)
1. Sun et al. (2020) — TTT with Self-Supervision. ICML 2020.
2. Finn et al. (2017) — MAML. ICML 2017.
3. Ba et al. (2016) — Fast Weights. NeurIPS 2016.
4. Katharopoulos et al. (2020) — Transformers are RNNs. ICML 2020.
5. Schlag et al. (2021) — Linear Transformers = Fast Weights. ICML 2021.
6. von Oswald et al. (2022/2023) — Transformers learn in-context by GD. ICML 2023.
7. Sun et al. (2024) — TTT-Layer (RNNs with Expressive Hidden States). arXiv 2024.
8. Behrouz et al. (2025) — Titans. arXiv 2025.
